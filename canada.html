Scholarships to Study in the USA

The United States offers rich merit-based and need-blind scholarship initiatives for international candidates, including the FulBright awards, specialized institutional fellowships, and athletic grants. Standard application prerequisites include:

1. Academic Transcripts & Standardized Tests

Certified historical grades matched alongside required SAT, ACT, GRE, or GMAT score files tracking performance benchmarks.

2. English Proficiency Verification

Updated score indicators from validated testing systems including IELTS, TOEFL, or Duolingo English examinations.

3. Statement of Purpose (SOP)

A personalized essay detailing your creative drives, academic focus, and clear long-term visions mapped into the university.

4. Letters of Recommendation (LOR)

Authoritative testimonials from former faculty guides or professional research leads detailing your analytical capacities.

FAQs

What is an I-20 Form, and how does Phebe Consultancy assist me in getting it?

The I-20 form is an official government document issued by a certified US institution confirming your full enrolment and financial viability status. It is required to pay your SEVIS fee and schedule your visa interview. Our team handles the direct university communications to verify your funding documents and accelerate I-20 delivery.

Can I work on-campus or off-campus while studying under an F-1 Student Visa?

F-1 visa status enables international students to work on-campus up to 20 hours per week during active semesters and full-time during official institutional breaks. Off-campus employment generally requires pre-authorization under specialized programs such as Curricular Practical Training (CPT) or Optional Practical Training (OPT) aligned with your study concentration.

What is OPT, and how does it help my post-graduation career opportunities?

Optional Practical Training (OPT) allows international graduates to work in the USA within their specific field for up to 12 months. Graduates from approved Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math (STEM) disciplines are eligible for an additional 24-month extension, offering up to 3 total years of professional work authorization.

How does Phebe Consultancy prepare me for the US Embassy visa interview?

The US visa evaluation places heavy emphasis on the in-person consulate interview. We conduct rigorous, structured mock interviews covering prospective questions regarding your institutional selections, academic intent, financial foundations, and clear ties to your home country to ensure absolute confidence.
