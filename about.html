Our Story

From a Dream to Nigeria's Trusted Travel Brand

Phebe Consultancy & Educational Management Services was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to make quality travel accessible to every Nigerian, regardless of budget or destination.

What started as a small office in Lagos has grown into a full-service travel agency serving thousands of travelers annually — from students heading abroad for education, to families planning dream vacations, to executives traveling for business.

Today, Phebe is recognized as one of Nigeria's most trusted travel consultancies, offering comprehensive services including flight bookings, visa assistance, travel packages, and bespoke travel planning.
