Documents Required

Common Visa Requirements

While requirements vary by country, most visa applications require these core documents. Our team guides you through every specific requirement.

1 Valid International Passport (minimum 6 months validity)
2 Bank Statements (last 3–6 months)
3 Proof of Employment / Business Registration
4 Hotel / Accommodation Bookings
5 Return Flight Booking Confirmation
6 Travel Insurance Certificate
7 Passport-sized Photographs (white background)

FAQ

Visa Questions Answered

Processing times vary by country. UAE visas can take 3–5 working days, UK visas 3–4 weeks, and US/Canada visas 4–8 weeks. We advise applying at least 6–8 weeks before your travel date.
No agency can legally guarantee visa approval, as the final decision rests with the embassy. However, our experienced team maximizes your chances by ensuring your application is complete, compelling, and error-free. Our approval rate is over 90%.
We analyze the rejection reason and advise on the best course of action, whether that's reapplying with stronger documentation, appealing the decision, or applying for a different visa type. We support you throughout the process.
