Can I work while studying in Australia?

You may choose to work while you study in Australia. You can earn an income on the side while growing academically and getting a taste of the local culture. Your working hours can go up to 48 hours every two weeks with unlimited working hours during holiday breaks.

Can I extend my stay in Australia after completing my studies?

Yes, it is possible to extend your stay in Australia. Whether you want to continue your studies or work, you can apply for a different visa accordingly. The most common step for international students completing undergraduate and postgraduate courses is to apply for a Graduate Work Visa, which allows them to stay in Australia for up to 4 years depending upon the course and location of study. Our consultant will help you find the best visa options based on your future plans.

Can I re-apply for an Australian visa in case it gets rejected?

Yes, do not worry that your visa got rejected. There are always opportunities available to lodge the same visa or a different visa based on your eligibility. Based on the reason for visa rejection and your circumstances, we can advise you about prospects. The immigration website and the documents sent along with the visa rejection explains that you have an opportunity to once again apply with the additional information required.

How can I get a full scholarship to study in Australia?

The grant of most scholarships to study in Australia is based on merit. These scholarships are offered by the Australian Government, education providers and other public and private organisations. The students have to demonstrate their eligibility for the scholarship applied. If you would like to check your eligibility for scholarships, contact our friendly team.
